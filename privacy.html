  1. User Provided Information. Company collects any information Users provide directly to Company. Certain information will be collected upon Account creation, Users’ purchase, using interactive areas of Company Services, signing up to receive Company communications, during the request of customer support, and during any other contact or communication with Company.

    Company may also collect and store information about other people and businesses that Users provide to Company, as well as information about you provided by others. If you or other users who use Services upload, share or distribute content, including, without limitation, videos, comments or any other submission, and such content contains personal information about you or others, such information may be stored in order to allow for such uploading, sharing or distribution. As a requirement to use our Services, you acknowledge that you have the appropriate consent from any individual or business to share their personal information with Company.

  2. Information Collected Automatically. Company may collect some information automatically when User accesses Services, this information may include:

    1. Logging Information: Company may log information about Users’ use of Services including but not limited to, Users’ browser type, browser plug-in details, operating system, device type, access times, time zone, usage data, referring/exit pages and URLs, User submitted preferences, number of clicks, pages viewed, date and time of each visitor request, screen size and related information, language preference, referring site User IP address and pages User visited immediately preceding the access of Services.

    2. Cookies and Tracking Devices: Company uses different technology to collect information including, but not limited to, sending cookies to User computers or mobile devices. Cookies are small data files stored on Users’ hard drive or in Users’ device memory that helps Company improve Services and the User experience. Company may use both persistent and session cookies; persistent cookies remain on your computer after you close your session and until you delete them, while session cookies expire when you close your browser.

      Company may also collect information using web beacons or tracking pixels. Web beacons are electronic images that may be used in our Services or emails to help deliver cookies, count visits, understand usage and campaign effectiveness, and determine whether an email has been opened and acted upon.

      Web browsers generally accept cookies by default. Users may choose to set, or alter, web browser preferences to remove or reject cookies. However, this may limit and affect the functionality of Company’s Services.

    3. Mobile Device Location Information. When you visit the Services from your mobile device, Company may collect and store your location information if Users’ mobile settings allow the submission and collection of such data. Collection of this information may improve User Services.

    4. Information Company Collects From Other Sources. Company may collect or obtain information from other sources and combine such information with that collected through Services.

    Company uses different technology to produce pop-ups on Services. Pop-ups are web browser windows that appear while User is engaged on a separate web browser window. Pop-ups may be used for links to other sites or pages of Company, various third party sites. Company may use pop-ups for any reason, at any time, with or without notice to User. Web browsers generally allow pop-ups by default. Users may choose to set, or alter, web browser preferences to remove or reject pop-ups. However, this may limit and affect the functionality of Company’s Services.

    Company Services uses Google Analytics. This program is a web based analytic software produced by Google. Company uses this program to track and report traffic on Company Services. Information collected from Analytics is used to later improve the experience of Users when using Company Services. By using Services, Users hereby acknowledge and consent to such information being collected by Analytics, with or without notice to User. Users hereby acknowledge and consent to Company’s collection, storage, and use of such information, without objection.
