Here is how to claim your interview..

To get your Devschool Interview we need you to help us run an experiement! Just open a new browser window, and google "online coding bootcamp" or for bonus points, "coding bootcamp".

Then click through the pages of results one by one until you find our link. Take a screeshot of the link position for us and then, click it! Once you have done that, you can then close the browser window. Make sure you don't hit the back button!

Send us a screenshot (interviews@devschool.rocks) of the position you found us at and tell us why you want to join Devschool.

And then, last step, like, follow and share either our facebook or twitter pages. This helps us spread the word and it also gives us a lot of insight as to who is looking at joining us.

Thanks for your interest in Devschool! We hope to play an important part in making your dreams come true!
